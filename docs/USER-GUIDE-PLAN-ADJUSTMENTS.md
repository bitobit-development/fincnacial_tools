# User Guide: Adjusting Your Retirement Plan

Welcome to the Plan Adjustments feature! This guide will help you understand how to customize your retirement plan parameters and see the impact of your changes.

## Overview

The AI Advisor has analyzed your financial situation and provided recommendations. However, you might want to adjust these recommendations based on your personal preferences, risk tolerance, or financial goals. The Plan Adjustments feature lets you customize three key parameters:

- **Monthly RA Contribution:** How much you contribute to your Retirement Annuity each month
- **Investment Return Rate:** Expected annual return on your investments
- **Inflation Rate:** Expected annual inflation rate

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Navigate to the Recommendations Tab

1. Open your web browser and go to the AI Advisor page
2. You'll see three tabs at the top: **Overview**, **Recommendations**, and **Chat**
3. Click on the **Recommendations** tab

You should now see the Plan Adjustments panel with three sliders.

Before making changes, take a moment to understand what you're seeing:

- Shows the difference in retirement nest egg and monthly drawdown

## Adjusting Your Plan

### Step 3: Modify Parameters

You can adjust any of the three parameters:

#### Monthly RA Contribution

**What it is:** The amount you contribute to your Retirement Annuity each month.

**Range:** R 0 to R 500,000

**How to adjust:**
1. Click and drag the slider left (decrease) or right (increase)
2. OR click on the number input and type a specific amount
3. See the impact update in real-time

**Example:**
- AI recommends: R 20,000/month
- You adjust to: R 25,000/month
- Impact: Higher retirement savings, but less money available now

**Considerations:**
- Can you afford this contribution with your current budget?
- Does it align with your other financial goals?
- Are you maxing out tax benefits?

#### Investment Return Rate

**What it is:** The expected annual return on your retirement investments.

**Range:** 0% to 15%

**How to adjust:**
1. Drag the slider to set your expected return
2. OR type a percentage directly

**Example:**
- AI recommends: 7.0% (conservative estimate)
- You adjust to: 8.5% (more optimistic)
- Impact: Higher projected retirement savings

**Considerations:**
- What's realistic based on your investment strategy?
- Are you comfortable with the risk required for higher returns?
- Have you researched historical returns for your asset mix?

**Warning:** Setting unrealistically high returns can lead to disappointing results. Be conservative in your estimates.

#### Inflation Rate

**What it is:** The expected annual increase in cost of living.

**Range:** 0% to 10%

**How to adjust:**
1. Move the slider to adjust inflation expectations
2. OR enter a specific percentage

**Example:**
- AI recommends: 6.0% (based on South African averages)
- You adjust to: 5.5% (more optimistic)
- Impact: Higher purchasing power in retirement

**Considerations:**
- What's the historical inflation rate in South Africa?
- Do you expect inflation to rise or fall in the coming decades?
- Are your expenses likely to track general inflation?

### Step 4: Review the Impact

As you adjust the sliders, the **Impact Summary** updates automatically:

**Nest Egg at Retirement:**
- Shows the difference between your adjustments and AI recommendations
- **Positive (+R 125,450):** Your adjustments lead to higher savings
- **Negative (-R 50,000):** Your adjustments lead to lower savings
- **Color coding:** Green for positive, red for negative

**Monthly Drawdown:**
- Shows how much you can withdraw each month in retirement
- **Positive (+R 1,250):** You'll have more to spend monthly
- **Negative (-R 500):** You'll have less to spend monthly
- **Color coding:** Green for positive, red for negative

**Example Impact:**
```
If you increase monthly contribution from R 20,000 to R 25,000
AND increase expected returns from 7% to 8.5%:

Nest Egg at Retirement:  +R 125,450 (vs AI recommendation)
Monthly Drawdown:        +R 1,250 (vs AI recommendation)
```

This means you'll have R 125,450 more saved and can withdraw R 1,250 more per month in retirement.

## Saving Your Adjustments

### Step 5: Save Your Changes

Once you're happy with your adjustments:

1. Click the **"Save Adjustments"** button at the bottom of the panel
2. Wait for the confirmation message: **"Adjustments saved successfully"**
3. The badge at the top will change from "Unsaved changes" to "Changes saved"

**What happens when you save:**
- Your custom values are stored in the database
- They persist across page refreshes and browser sessions
- The AI chatbot will use these values in future recommendations
- You can return later and see your saved values

**Important:** You MUST click "Save Adjustments" for changes to persist. Simply moving the sliders doesn't save anything.

### Step 6: Using the AI Chatbot with Your Adjustments

After saving your adjustments, you can chat with the AI advisor:

1. Click on the **Chat** tab
2. Ask questions about your retirement plan
3. The AI will use your custom values in its responses

**Example conversation:**

> **You:** How much will I have saved when I retire?

> **AI:** Based on your adjusted monthly contribution of R 25,000 and expected investment return of 8.5%, you'll have approximately R 4.5 million saved when you retire at age 65. This is higher than the initial projection due to your increased contribution and return expectations.

Notice how the AI acknowledges your custom values ("your adjusted monthly contribution of R 25,000").

## Resetting to AI Recommendations

### Step 7: Reset to AI Values (Optional)

If you want to go back to the AI's original recommendations:

1. Click the **"Reset to AI"** button
2. All sliders will move to the AI-recommended positions
3. The badge will show "Unsaved changes"
4. Review the new values
5. Click **"Save Adjustments"** to make the reset permanent

**When to reset:**
- You're not happy with your custom values
- You want to see the AI's original recommendations again
- You've been experimenting and want to start fresh

**Note:** Resetting doesn't automatically save. You must click "Save Adjustments" to persist the reset.

## Tips for Effective Planning

### Be Realistic

- Use conservative estimates for investment returns
- Factor in market volatility and economic uncertainty
- Don't overcommit to contributions you can't sustain

### Review Regularly

- Revisit your adjustments every 6-12 months
- Adjust as your financial situation changes
- Update when life events occur (marriage, children, inheritance)

### Balance Risk and Reward

- Higher returns require more risk
- Ensure your risk tolerance matches your expectations
- Diversify investments to manage risk

### Consider Multiple Scenarios

- Try different combinations of parameters
- See how optimistic vs. pessimistic scenarios affect your retirement
- Plan for the worst, hope for the best

### Use the AI Chatbot

- Ask "what if" questions after saving adjustments
- Request explanations for why certain values are recommended
- Get personalized advice based on your custom parameters

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: Save button is grayed out

**Cause:** No unsaved changes detected.

**Solution:** Move at least one slider to a different position, or your current values match saved values.

### Problem: My changes disappeared after refreshing

**Cause:** You didn't click "Save Adjustments" before leaving the page.

**Solution:** Make your adjustments again and remember to save.

### Problem: The AI isn't using my adjusted values

**Cause:** Either you didn't save, or there's a session issue.

**Solution:**
1. Check that the badge shows "Changes saved" or "Modified"
2. Try refreshing the page to reload your session
3. If still not working, contact support

### Problem: I can't move the slider to a specific value

**Cause:** The value is outside the allowed range.

**Solution:** Check the min/max limits for each parameter:
- Monthly RA: R 0 - R 500,000
- Investment Return: 0% - 15%
- Inflation: 0% - 10%

### Problem: Impact summary shows "N/A" or "Error"

**Cause:** Calculation error or missing data.

**Solution:**
1. Refresh the page
2. Ensure all parameters are within valid ranges
3. If persists, contact support

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I adjust parameters multiple times?

Yes! Adjust as many times as you like. Each save overwrites previous adjustments.

### Will the AI remember my adjustments forever?

Your adjustments are saved in your session. As long as you use the same session ID, they'll be remembered. Sessions typically last until you explicitly start a new one.

### Can I have different adjustments for different scenarios?

Currently, you can only save one set of adjustments per session. To explore multiple scenarios, you'll need to manually change and save each time.

### Do my adjustments affect other users?

No. Your adjustments are private to your session and don't affect anyone else.

### What if I want to go back to the AI's recommendations?

Click "Reset to AI" and then "Save Adjustments".

### How accurate are the projections?

Projections are estimates based on your inputs. Actual results will vary due to market performance, life changes, and economic conditions. Use conservative estimates.

### Can I adjust other parameters not shown on the sliders?

Currently, only three parameters are adjustable. Other aspects of your profile (age, current savings, etc.) are fixed based on your initial input.

### What happens if I close the browser without saving?

Unsaved changes are lost. Always click "Save Adjustments" before leaving.

### Can I export or print my adjusted plan?

Not currently, but you can take screenshots or ask the AI chatbot to summarize your plan.

### Who can I contact if I need help?

For technical issues or questions, contact support through the main application menu.
